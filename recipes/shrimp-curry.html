Shrimp Curry

Know your dish ...

    Indian Shrimp Curry made with coconut milk, tomato sauce and warm Indian spices
    is a quick 20 minute curry dish you can enjoy any day of the week! This Shrimp Curry
    throws all the preconceptions of how long and difficult curries are out the window.
    The spices are very easy to find at your local grocery store!
    Serve with basmati rice or an Indian flatbread!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • ⅔ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground red chile pepper
  • 2 pounds medium shrimp - peeled and deveined
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • chopped fresh cilantro to taste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 10 fresh curry leaves (Optional)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1.             Heat the oil in a wok or large saucepan over nearly high heat.
            Add the onions; cook and stir until browned. Mix in the curry leaves,
            then season with the ginger garlic paste, coriander and salt. Cook and 
            stir for 1 minute.
  2.             Season with salt and turmeric, then mix in the tomato, chile powder,
            shrimp and water. Reduce the heat to medium-high and cook for 7 to 8
            minutes, until shrimp are opaque. Taste and adjust salt and chile powder
            if necessary. Season with garam masala, stir and remove from the heat.
        
  3.             Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with flat bread or rice.
